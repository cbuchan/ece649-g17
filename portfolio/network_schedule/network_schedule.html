Simulation Analysis


Running the simulation with bandwidth of 60kbps, we obtain the results listed below. Multiplying the network bandwidth by the utilization gives us the actual bandwidth used:
    200 * 0.7512 = 150.24kbps
This is between the best- and worst-case values predicted by our analysis. Factors which might cause simluation results to differ include different ordering of events, and different tripping of smart sensors.
